    BOM¶

    Go through this list and cross off anything you have. Then add up what is left is what your printer will cost.

    Things that are not listed here are 3 partial spools of filament, your time, patience and last remaining hairs.

    The purchasing suggestions here will have leftover parts to start your next project.

    You may find some parts cheaper on Aliexpress if you want to wait a month.

    Part Prep¶

    Cut the 5mm rod into these lengths:

    • Rod 1: 220mm + 60mm + 3x 40mm
    • Rod 2: 220mm + 3x 40mm + 2x 30mm
    • Rod 3: 5x40mm + 2x15mm
